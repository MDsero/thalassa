Our Craft

Three decades of hulls, laid one at a time.

Thalassa began in a single dry dock with two shipwrights and a belief that a boat's character is set long before it touches water — in the fairing of a hull, the temper of a weld, the grain matched across a saloon table.

Today our yard builds fewer than forty vessels a year, by design. Every keel is still laid by hand, every sea trial run by the engineer who specified the systems.

1994Founded in a single dry dock, three hulls a year.
2006First carbon-hull performance line launched.
2015Hybrid propulsion introduced across the fleet.
2024Aurelia superyacht series debuts at 118 ft.
